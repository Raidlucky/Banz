Commands

  • >join {joins the voice channel your in]
  • >playlists [gives list of playlists to try with the bot]
  • >toggle (command) [turns on or off command]
  • >play (song) [plays song and playlists]
  • >help [gives commands]
  • >contact [shows bot developers]
  • >poll [creates a poll]
  • >volume [changes the bot 's volume]
  • >stop [stops playing music and leaves]
  • >loop [loops the current song]
  • >clear clears the current queue
  • >queue [gives info on the queue]
  • >shuffle [shuffles the current queue]
  • >prefix [changes the bot 's prefix]
  • >leave [makes the bot leave your channel]
